A Year That
Changed Everything

At 18, I made the decision to leave Belgium alone and spend a full academic year at Mesa Ridge High School in Colorado Springs, living with an American host family, far from everything familiar.

Immersed in an entirely English-speaking environment, I attended classes alongside American students, joined school activities, and navigated daily life in a culture very different from my own. It was challenging, enriching, and ultimately transformative.

This experience gave me far more than language fluency, it built my independence, my ability to connect with people from all backgrounds, and a genuine curiosity for the world. It is the foundation of everything I bring to international contexts today.
